📊 Superstore Sales & Profitability Analysis:

📌 Objective:

Analyze retail sales data to identify key factors affecting profitability and provide actionable business insights.

🛠 Tools Used:

• Excel

• Pivot Tables

• Data Cleaning

• Dashboard Design

📂 Dataset:

Superstore sales dataset containing information on sales, profit, discount, category, subcategories and region.
Raw dataset included as superstore_raw_data.csv

📈 Key Insights:

• Furniture drives high revenue but delivers only ~2% profit margin

• The Central region consistently underperforms across categories

• Profit starts declining rapidly when discounts exceed 20%

• The combination of Furniture + high discounts is the biggest loss driver

💡 Recommendations:

• Reduce excessive discounting in Furniture category.

• Optimize pricing strategy in low-performing regions like Central.

• By capping discounts and optimizing pricing in Furniture, especially in the Central region, profitability can be significantly improved without increasing sales volume.
